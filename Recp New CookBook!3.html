About TONIGHT

TONIGHT is very special because it is designed specifically to provide
recipes that you can make tonight, with whatever you’ve got on hand,
no matter how rushed you are, catering for all budgets, tastes and
levels of cooking. There’s 150 brand new recipes, plus 800 variations
on those recipes and 3,000 possible combinations using mix-and
-match formulas!

Some dinners can be cooked in 20 minutes, and others can be made
using only pantry staples – make dinner right now without going to the
store! For high-impact guest-impressing, turn to Sunday Suppers while
the Sweet chapter delivers on the promise of maximum decadence
with minimum fuss.

There’s a chapter filled with recipes using just one-pot/tray, an entire
chapter dedicated to my beloved stir fry sauce, Charlie. And my
ultimate cheat sheet for how to roast (almost) any vegetable – plus 25
different ways to serve them – is now permanently stuck on my fridge.







But the chapter I am most excited about is My Favourites Made Easier
which is filled with midweek versions of some of my favourite dishes.
Aussie Meat Pies on Monday night. 20 minute Hainanese Chicken. Fully
loaded Burritos made using just one pot, a one hour luxurious stew,
and a dump-and-bake Satay Chicken which I’m betting will be the
single most popular recipe in the whole book.


And – Dozer! It wouldn’t be a book I authored if there wasn’t plenty of
Dozer throughout the book. There have even been whisperings that I
only made this book as an excuse to shamelessly flaunt him….I plead
the fifth!


I’m so proud of TONIGHT. I hope you and your family enjoy cooking and
eating from it as much as I loved making it! – Nagi x
